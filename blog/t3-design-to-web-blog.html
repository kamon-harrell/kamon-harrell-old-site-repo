  • What is a responsive site, and why is responsiveness important?

    A responsive site it one that changes the content based on the size of the screen, or device you are using. This could mean eliminating elements found on a larger page, or stacking content to look nice on a smaller screen.

  • What is mobile first design, and why is it important?

    Mobile first is a design method used when creating responsive web sites and content. If you start a design using a limited screen size such as a mobile device, then you are forced to focus on whats most important, and how to make an easy to use interface. This translates perfectly for making the desktop sites, as you can focus on adding new content, as opposed to stripping it away.

  • What are frameworks, and what are their pros and cons?

    Frameworks are almost like blueprints you can use and edit to make creating websites and other programs a bit easier, and much faster. Instead of reinventing the wheel, you can simply buy a premade wheel, and make adjustments accordingly.
