  • What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

    They are relatively the same! Being self aware is being conscious of your current state, and being mindfull is the practice of bringing ones attention back to a center. Both of these practices are very complimentary, and some could argue are one in the same.

  • Which practise did I try between the body scan and journaling, and what was my experience of it?

    I tried the body scan, and found it to be very relaxing, so relaxing in fact that it put me to sleep! Regardless of it putting me to sleep, I tried this again this morning and once again found it profoundly relaxing, and i can see how this would be a good practice to get into for the start of a meditation session. I would also like to try the journaling exercise, as the research behind it is convincing enough to give it a go.

  • What did I take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

    I agreed with this section wholeheartedly. Being able to differentiate ourselves from our emotions is very important, otherwise we are creatures that react based on instinct, instead of thoughtful reflection.
