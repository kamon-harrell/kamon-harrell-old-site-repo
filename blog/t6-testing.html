  • What is scope?

    Scop in javascript is an interesting thing. In most programming languages scope keeps most thing inside their respective functions, meaning they can only be used be a very specific chunk of the code. This keeps variables and other defined things from interacting with other, and creating a mess of the program. Javescript does things a little bit different by allowing variables to be interacted with outside of their specific functions. For example, I could have a variable inside of a function, and i could change the value of that variable by redefining its value inside of another function or out side of a function.
